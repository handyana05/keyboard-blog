## FAQ

### Are mechanical keyboards good for typing?
Yes. Many typists prefer mechanical keyboards because of better feedback, durability, and comfort during long sessions.

### Are mechanical keyboards too loud?
Not necessarily. Linear or tactile switches without click mechanisms can be relatively quiet.

### Do I need an expensive keyboard for typing?
No. Many budget and mid-range mechanical keyboards provide excellent typing experiences.
